Guide to the sPEPs integrative

How to perform search

Clicking "Search" on the navigation menu bring you to the search webpage.

You may type or paste the sequence in the textarea marked in red.

Then, click on the "Submit" button marked in red.

You would be informed by message below ① the button, once the query is submitted to the engine.

And the results will start showing up ②.

Results Table

The webpage will update the result until all of them are displayed in the table.

You might scroll down to check the results

You could also scrol left or right to check more knowledge about the resulting sequence.

Once finished, your could download the result in comma separated format clicking the button marked in red.

You could also start the next query by clicking on this button (marked in red).

Check Available Databases

You could click the dropdown to see which sPEP database is available.
